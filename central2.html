A Message from the Principal

Welcome to Cusmaan Binu Cafaan Secondary School.

It is with great pride that I welcome you to Cusmaan Binu Cafaan Secondary School. Our school is proudly named after the esteemed third Caliph, Uthman ibn Affan (Cusmaan Binu Cafaan), a leader known as Dhun-Nurayn — the Possessor of Two Lights. This noble title is the very cornerstone of our educational philosophy.

We are dedicated to nurturing students who are guided by these two lights: the eternal light of faith, ethics, and integrity, and the brilliant light of academic knowledge and critical inquiry. With caring teachers and a strong partnership with families, we foster an environment that is intellectually challenging and spiritually nurturing. Our aim is to develop resilient, compassionate, and wise young adults who are prepared not only to excel in their chosen fields but also to lead lives of purpose and service.

Ahmed Bihi Abdi
Principal

Curriculum (Forms 1–4)

Clear, balanced subjects. Strong foundations for exams, further study, and character.

Chemistry

Matter, reactions, acids & bases, stoichiometry, and lab safety with hands-on investigations.

Biology

Cells to systems, genetics, ecology, and human health through observation and inquiry.

Physics

Motion, forces, energy, waves, and electricity—modelling the world with maths and experiments.

Mathematics

Number, algebra, geometry, trigonometry, probability & statistics with regular problem-solving.

History & Geography

Key eras, themes, and places—cause & effect, maps, fieldwork, and global awareness.

Islamic Studies

Qur’an, Fiqh, Akhlaq—character, reflection, and daily practice rooted in faith.

Notes: Coursework, quizzes, and practicals are used throughout the term. Extension and support are provided through clubs, tutorials, and lab time.
