Posted on August 15, 2025

Annual Science Fair Showcases Student Innovation

The school grounds were buzzing with excitement last Friday for our Annual Science Fair. Students from all forms presented a wide array of remarkable projects, demonstrating months of hard work, research, and ingenuity. The event was a testament to our commitment to fostering scientific inquiry and critical thinking.

Projects ranged from detailed models of renewable energy systems for Hargeisa to impressive studies on local biodiversity and water conservation techniques. A panel of judges, including teachers and local university science faculty, were thoroughly impressed with the quality and depth of the students' work.

Posted on August 12, 2025

Debate Team Wins Regional Championship

We are incredibly proud to announce that the Cusmaan Binu Cafaan Debate Team has won the first-place trophy at the Hargeisa Regional Debate Championship. After weeks of rigorous preparation and several rounds of intense competition against sixteen other schools, our team emerged victorious.

The final debate centered on the topic of sustainable urban development in the Horn of Africa. Our students presented well-researched arguments with clarity, confidence, and respect for their opponents, earning high praise from the judges.

Posted on August 11, 2025

School Celebrates Somali Language and Culture Week

Last week, our school hosted a vibrant and educational Somali Language and Culture Week. The event was dedicated to celebrating our rich heritage, language, and traditions. Throughout the week, students participated in various activities, including poetry recitations (gabay), traditional storytelling sessions, and a cultural dress day.

Special assemblies featured guest speakers who spoke on the importance of preserving the Somali language for future generations. The celebration concluded with a showcase of traditional food and crafts, prepared and presented by students and their families. It was a wonderful opportunity for our community to connect with its roots and share our culture with pride.

Posted on August 5, 2025

Students Lead Successful Community Clean-up Drive

Embodying our school's values of service and civic responsibility, over 50 student volunteers from all forms dedicated their Saturday to a community clean-up drive. Organized by the Student Council, the event focused on beautifying public parks and streets in our local neighborhood.

Armed with gloves and bags, students worked enthusiastically to collect litter, clear overgrown weeds, and plant new saplings in designated areas. The initiative was met with appreciation from local residents, many of whom came out to thank the students for their hard work.

Posted on July 28, 2025

Blue House Triumphs at Annual Sports Day

Our Annual Sports Day was held last week under clear skies, filled with thrilling competition and a wonderful display of school spirit. Students participated in a wide range of track and field events, showcasing their athletic talents and teamwork.

After a full day of impressive performances, Blue House accumulated the most points to win the championship trophy. The day concluded with a ceremony where medals were awarded to individual winners and the house captains of Blue House proudly accepted the overall prize.

Posted on July 22, 2025

Parent-Teacher Conferences Announced for First Term

We would like to inform all parents and guardians that the Parent-Teacher Conferences for the first term have been scheduled for the first week of September. This is an important opportunity to discuss your child's academic progress, classroom conduct, and overall development with their teachers.

A detailed schedule and instructions for booking appointments will be sent home with students next week. We strongly encourage all parents to attend to ensure a strong partnership between home and school in supporting our students' success. We look forward to seeing you there.
