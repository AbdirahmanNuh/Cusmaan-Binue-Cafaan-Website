Blue House Triumphs at a Thrilling Annual Sports Day

The school grounds were filled with energy, cheering, and outstanding displays of athleticism during our Annual Sports Day. A day of thrilling competition and excellent sportsmanship concluded with Blue House being crowned the champions for 2025 after a neck-and-neck race for the top spot.

Students from all four houses—Blue, Red, Green, and Yellow—competed with great enthusiasm in a wide range of track and field events. From the 100-meter dash to the long jump and team relays, every event was a testament to the hard work and preparation of our students.

Celebrating Participation and Excellence

While Blue House took home the final trophy, the day was a celebration of participation for all. The principal commended the students not only for their athletic achievements but also for the incredible spirit of support and encouragement they showed for their peers, regardless of house affiliation.

"Sports Day is about more than just winning," he stated during the closing ceremony. "It's about discipline, teamwork, and learning to both win with humility and lose with grace. I am immensely proud of every student who participated today."

Congratulations to Blue House and to all our students for making this year's Sports Day a resounding success!
