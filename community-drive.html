Students Lead Successful Community Clean-up Drive

Embodying our school's core value of service, over 50 student volunteers from all forms participated in a highly successful community clean-up day this past weekend. The event was organized by the Student Council in partnership with local community leaders to help beautify and maintain public spaces near our campus.

Armed with gloves, bags, and a tremendous amount of enthusiasm, our students worked for several hours, demonstrating a powerful commitment to civic responsibility. Their efforts were praised by local residents and officials who stopped by to offer their thanks.

A Day of Impact

The drive focused on the main park and surrounding streets, resulting in a cleaner and safer environment for everyone. Key achievements from the day include:

  • Over 20 large bags of litter and recyclables were collected and properly disposed of.
  • Garden beds in the local park were weeded and prepared for new seasonal flowers.
  • Several public benches were washed and cleaned.

We are incredibly proud of our students for taking initiative and making a tangible, positive impact on their community. This event is a wonderful example of how our values extend beyond the classroom walls.
