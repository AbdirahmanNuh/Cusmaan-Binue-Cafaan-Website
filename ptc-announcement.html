Announcement: Parent-Teacher Conferences for Term 1

As we approach the mid-point of our first term, we are pleased to announce the schedule for our upcoming Parent-Teacher Conferences. Parents and guardians are cordially invited to meet with teachers to discuss their child's academic progress, classroom engagement, and overall development.

These one-on-one meetings are a vital part of our home-school partnership, providing a valuable opportunity to collaborate for the success of our students. We strongly encourage all parents to attend.

Conference Schedule and Booking

  • Dates: Wednesday, October 29, 2025 to Friday, October 31, 2025.
  • Time: Appointments will be available from 01:00 PM to 04:00 PM each day.
  • Location: Main school building classrooms.

To schedule your appointments, please contact the main school office by phone or in person during regular school hours. Booking will be open from Monday, October 20th. We recommend booking early to secure your preferred times.

We look forward to seeing you and working together to support our students' learning journey.
