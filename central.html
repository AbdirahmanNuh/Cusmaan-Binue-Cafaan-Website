About Us

Welcome to Cusmaan Binu Cafaan Secondary School, where we believe that a truly great education builds both the mind and the soul. Our school provides a safe, supportive, and intellectually stimulating environment for students to thrive academically, personally, and spiritually.

Named in honour of the esteemed companion Uthman ibn Affan (Cusmaan Binu Cafaan), we are guided by a legacy of leadership, wisdom, and deep-seated integrity. Our dedicated staff is committed to delivering a challenging curriculum that prepares students for the competitive demands of university and the modern world, while seamlessly integrating core Islamic values into their daily learning experience.

Principal’s Message

Education at Cusmaan Binu Cafaan Secondary School is more than information; it shapes character. Guided by the legacy of Uthman ibn Affan (Dhun-Nurayn), we strive to unite two lights: the enduring light of faith, ethics, and integrity, and the bright light of academic knowledge and inquiry.

With caring teachers and a strong partnership with families, we foster an environment that is intellectually challenging and spiritually nurturing. Our aim is resilient, compassionate, and wise young people—ready to excel in their studies and to live with purpose and service.

Each day, this vision appears in focused lessons, respectful conduct, and steady routines that help students grow. We invite you to read the full message to see how our “Two Lights” philosophy guides everything we do.

About Us

At Cusmaan Binu Cafaan Secondary School (Forms 1–4), learning is calm and purposeful. We use simple routines, clear goals, and caring teachers to guide every student. We work closely with families and build everyday habits—respect, responsibility, punctuality, and service—so students can focus and do their best.

Lessons are practical and balanced. Students learn through practice, labs, projects, and regular feedback. They grow in English, Arabic, and Somali, and Qur’an/Islamic Studies shape good character each day. After class, clubs, competitions, and community service build confidence and leadership. By graduation, our students are exam-ready, skill-ready, and future-ready.

A Message from the Principal

Welcome to Cusmaan Binu Cafaan Secondary School.

It is with great pride and a profound sense of responsibility that I welcome you to the official portal of Cusmaan Binu Cafaan Secondary School.

At our institution, we believe that true education is not merely the acquisition of information, but the illumination of the soul and the cultivation of character. Our school is proudly named after the esteemed third Caliph, Uthman ibn Affan (Cusmaan Binu Cafaan), a leader known as Dhun-Nurayn — the Possessor of Two Lights. This noble title is the very cornerstone of our educational philosophy.

We are dedicated to nurturing students who are guided by these two lights: the eternal light of faith, ethics, and integrity, and the brilliant light of academic knowledge, critical inquiry, and worldly understanding. It is the perfect harmony of these two lights that creates a truly balanced and enlightened individual.

Education at Cusmaan Binu Cafaan Secondary School is more than information; it shapes character. Guided by the legacy of Uthman ibn Affan (Dhun-Nurayn), we strive to unite two lights: the enduring light of faith, ethics, and integrity, and the bright light of academic knowledge and inquiry.

With caring teachers and a strong partnership with families, we foster an environment that is intellectually challenging and spiritually nurturing. Our aim is resilient, compassionate, and wise young people—ready to excel in their studies and to live with purpose and service.

Our dedicated faculty is committed to fostering an environment that is both intellectually challenging and spiritually nurturing. We see education as a sacred trust and a collaborative partnership between the school, our students, and their families. Together, we work to develop resilient, compassionate, and wise young adults who are prepared not only to excel in their chosen fields but also to lead lives of purpose and service.

I warmly invite you to explore our community, to witness our commitment to excellence firsthand, and to discover how we are preparing the leaders of tomorrow for a future where they will not only succeed, but also serve and inspire.

Ahmed Bihi Abdi
Principal
