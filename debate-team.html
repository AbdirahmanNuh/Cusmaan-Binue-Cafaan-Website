Debate Team Wins Regional Championship

With pride and excitement, we announce that our talented debate team has brought home the first-place trophy from the Inter-School Regional Championship. After weeks of rigorous preparation, research, and practice, their hard work culminated in a series of powerful and persuasive performances against top schools from across the region.

A Hard-Fought Victory

The final round was a gripping contest, with our team arguing passionately on the topic of sustainable development. Their well-reasoned arguments, eloquent delivery, and exceptional teamwork ultimately won over the judges, securing a unanimous victory.

The team captain, speaking after the event, thanked the coaches and fellow students for their unwavering support. "This victory isn't just for us; it's for the entire school community that believed in us," she said. "It proves that with dedication and collaboration, we can achieve great things."

Congratulations to our debate team for this outstanding achievement and for representing Cusmaan Binu Cafaan Secondary School with such distinction and integrity.
