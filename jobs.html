Mathematics Teacher

Full-timeMain Campus, Hargeisa

Position Overview

Cusmaan Binu Cafaan Secondary School is seeking a passionate and qualified Mathematics Teacher to join our faculty. The ideal candidate will be adept at teaching a range of mathematical concepts to secondary school students (Forms 1-4) and will be committed to fostering an engaging and supportive learning environment that aligns with our school's core values.

Key Responsibilities

  • Plan, prepare, and deliver high-quality mathematics lessons that cater to students of different abilities.
  • Assess and monitor student progress through homework, quizzes, and exams, providing constructive feedback.
  • Create a positive and structured classroom environment that encourages critical thinking and student participation.
  • Collaborate with other teachers and staff to improve the overall educational experience for our students.

Qualifications and Experience

  • Bachelor's degree in Mathematics, Education, or a related field. A valid teaching certification is required.
  • Minimum of 2-3 years of secondary school mathematics teaching experience.
  • Strong knowledge of the secondary mathematics curriculum and effective pedagogical strategies.
  • Excellent communication, organizational, and classroom management skills.

How to Apply

Interested candidates should email their CV and a cover letter to careers@cusmancafaan.edu with the subject line: Application: Mathematics Teacher - [Your Full Name].

English Teacher

Full-timeMain Campus, Hargeisa

Position Overview

We are looking for a dynamic English Teacher to inspire a love of language and literature in our students. The successful candidate will be responsible for teaching English grammar, composition, and literature to students in Forms 1-4, helping them develop strong communication and analytical skills.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design and deliver engaging lesson plans that cover reading, writing, speaking, and listening skills.
  • Introduce students to a variety of literary genres and encourage critical analysis of texts.
  • Assess student work, providing timely and effective feedback to support their development.
  • Prepare students for national English language and literature examinations.

Qualifications and Experience

  • Bachelor's degree in English, Literature, Education, or a related field, along with a teaching certification.
  • Minimum of 2-3 years of experience teaching English at the secondary level.
  • Excellent command of the English language, both written and spoken.
  • Ability to inspire and motivate students from diverse backgrounds.

How to Apply

Interested candidates should email their CV and a cover letter to careers@cusmancafaan.edu with the subject line: Application: English Teacher - [Your Full Name].

Lab Technician

Part-timeScience Department

Position Overview

Our Science Department is looking for a detail-oriented and reliable Lab Technician to support our practical science curriculum. This part-time role is essential for ensuring our science labs are safe, well-stocked, and ready for hands-on learning in Biology, Chemistry, and Physics.

Key Responsibilities

  • Prepare equipment, materials, and chemical solutions for science experiments.
  • Maintain, clean, and organize all laboratory equipment and apparatus.
  • Manage the inventory of chemicals and lab supplies, and assist with ordering new stock.
  • Ensure that all lab activities adhere to strict health and safety regulations.
  • Provide technical assistance to teachers and students during practical lab sessions.

Qualifications and Experience

  • A diploma or degree in a relevant scientific field (e.g., Chemistry, Biology, Laboratory Technology).
  • At least 1-2 years of experience working in a school or research laboratory setting.
  • Strong knowledge of laboratory safety protocols and procedures.
  • Excellent organizational skills and attention to detail.

How to Apply

Interested candidates should email their CV and a cover letter to careers@cusmancafaan.edu with the subject line: Application: Lab Technician - [Your Full Name].
